How to use this site

A suggested order. Skip around once you know the material.

1

Read the chapters

Each of the 19 official sub-objectives has a Learn tab (what the exam tests, key services), a Decide tab (clue words, decision tables, common traps), and a Drill tab (scenario practice). Open chapters →

2

Look up services

A quick reference of every GCP service that shows up on the exam, grouped by category, with "best for" hints and decision pointers. Also includes real-world case studies. Open services →

3

Work through Google's 26 sample questions

The official sample with a step-by-step how to think walkthrough for each question: clue words to underline, option-by-option elimination, mental model to lock in. Open official sample →

4

Drill exam traps

All scenario drills across every chapter, searchable and filterable by topic. Each card shows the temptation, the right call, and why the other options fail. Open exam traps →

5

Confirm against the official exam guide

Google's PDF — the source of truth for what's tested. Bookmark it and come back when you want to verify a chapter is still in scope. Open exam guide →

Tip — on iOS, "Add to Home Screen" from the share menu installs this site as an offline app.
